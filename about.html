About Me

I am Gideon Polycarp. A Nigerian born and brought up in Benin Republic, into a family of six (6). Impacting lives have been my passion and lot of lives have been impacted in few years. I'm an Economist, a teacher, a motivational speaker and a writer.

Vision


My vision is to re-invent the mindset of teenagers and youths (foundation builders), teaching them on how to train and invest in their mind.

The mind is like an incubator, it gives you back what has been deposited in it. Its outcome is from the combination of things it has received. Your mindset is what tells who you are. "As a man thinketh so he is (Proverb 23 vs 7)". It is said that there is no difference between the rich and poor, they both are human but their mindset differentiate them. Success doesn't come from the outside, it comes from within. Success is first conceived in the mind and passes through the street of the mind before it gets to the sight of man.
