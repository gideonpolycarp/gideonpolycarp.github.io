Make your birthday count: an important thing in life

  • Written by Gideon Polycarp
  • @Gideon

    • Every birth year should have goals and cause of actions or steps for achieving these goals. You have just 365 days in a year ...
    • Live each day with a purpose. Don't waste your time watching television and videos. Those you are watching have put thier best into what you are watching. Change the position and you will be watched.
