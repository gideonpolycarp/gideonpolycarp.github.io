Is your birthday worth celebrating: a must answered question

  • Written by Gideon Polycarp
  • @Gideon

    • Happy Birthday! you're plus one (+1) today, to God be the glory. Birthday wishes and gifts from friends, family and well wishers. But the question is: "IS YOUR BIRTHDAY WORTH CELEBRATING ?". What impacts have you made? What impact are you making ? and what impact will you make? What exactly makes people in your little corner happy that you were born?

    On the day you were born, a solution to a particular problem was born. You may have been born as a parental accident but you're not a divine accident. You may have been born out of wedlock but you were not born out of God's knowledge. God knew you were coming and He made sufficient provisions for you to fulfil your divine purpose. You're a solution to a problem in your family, environment and society.

    How can you celebrate a new birthyear when the previous one was spent with no achievement and purpose. There's a problem you were born to solve, you've not discovered the problem and you celebrate a new birthyear??. The children of Israel continued suffering in Egypt until Moses arose and lived out purpose. The people of Israel were intimidated repeatly by Goliath until David arose and lived out his purpose. The Jews in Shiloh would have all been wiped off, thank God for Esther who lived out her purpose. Time would fail to talk about Thomas Edison, Mary Slessor, Abraham Graham Bell. All these people are celebrated till date.

    When you discover your purpose and love it out, your birthday will be celebrated now and even after your death. Are you an asset or liability in your family and society? What makes people happy you were born? Don't celebrate birthday if all these questions can't be answered.
